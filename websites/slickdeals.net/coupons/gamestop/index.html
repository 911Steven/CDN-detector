GameStop is a global video game and consumer electronics retailer with more than 6,000 stores around the globe. Featuring a vast selection of new and pre-owned video games, consoles and other products for tech enthusiasts, GameStop keeps popular gaming products and electronics accessible and affordable.

GameStop Discount Tips

The great thing about GameStop is that you can trade in your old games, gaming consoles, smartphones and tablets for either cash or credit towards your next purchase. It’s a good way to get rid of those games you’re done playing or electronics you don’t need, while also earning money toward the newest releases.

Serious gamers should also sign up for GameStop’s PowerUp Rewards program, which earns you points on every dollar you spend, as well as on trade-ins. Plus, if you upgrade to the PowerUp Pro program (for $15), you’ll get 10% more points and extra credit on trade-ins, as well as buy-two-get-one-free pre-owned games, exclusive savings offers and a year’s subscription to Game Informer magazine. Not a bad deal for the price.

For everyday savings at GameStop, check out their weekly ad for dollar amount-off deals in the range of $10 to $30 off. They also frequently have special offers when you trade in certain gaming consoles, like $100 off the purchase of a new PS4 when you trade in your old PS3, for example. Look out for free shipping promotion codes as well, or use their free in-store pickup option to save on shipping costs. Bookmark this page and we’ll let you know about all the best deals and promo codes GameStop has to offer.

While opting for pre-owned games and equipment is a sure way to save at GameStop, don’t forget about their download program as well, which gives you access to more than 1,500 PC games at deep discounts, sometimes up to 75% off. You’ll get your game instantly without even leaving the house.
