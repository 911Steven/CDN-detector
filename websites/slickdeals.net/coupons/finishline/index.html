Finish Line strives to bring top quality athletic footwear and apparel to shoppers, and create a premium shopping experience while they’re at it. With more than 660 stores nationwide, it’s easy to find the best sneakers for you in terms of fit, function and affordability.

Finish Line Discount Tips

Finish Line has a great selection of mark downs, with hundreds of shoes frequently discounted to the tune of 40%. Plus, they offer free shipping on orders more than $100. They also run regular spend and save coupons (typically around $10 off orders of $100 or more) and percentage off discounts. You can usually find the best deals on end of season products just before new models come out. If you’ve got your eye on a new pair of kicks, wait it out until the end of the season and you might be rewarded with a great deal. Bookmark this page to stay up to date on all the best coupons Finish Line has to offer. Additionally, be sure to sign up for Finish Line emails and they’ll send you coupons and news about the latest shoes.

If you’re a real footwear fanatic, be sure to join the free Winner’s Circle rewards program. You’ll earn points on every purchase you make at Finish Line, and once you hit 200 points, you’ll get a $20 reward. (Just note that rewards certificates can only be combined with one other coupon.) Members are also sent exclusive coupons and free shipping offers, and they get early access to new arrivals. To stay on top of your rewards points, download the Finish Line app. It’ll keep everything organized for you in terms of rewards, and send you notifications on the latest shoe releases.
