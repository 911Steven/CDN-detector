Cabela’s has been outfitting hunting, fishing and outdoor enthusiasts with a wide and distinct selection of outdoor gear for more than 50 years. With more than a 100 specialized catalogs per year, a thriving website, and brick-and-mortar locations that are part retail and part wildlife museum, it’s easy to see why Cabela’s is a household name when it comes to outdoor products.

Cabelas Discount Tips

It’s easy to save money at Cabela’s. With regular seasonal and holiday sales, you can expect to see 20% to 50% off various departments throughout the year. Look for things like fishing gear to go on sale in the spring, winter shoe and apparel discounts in late fall, and other seasonally appropriate sales.

Cabela’s sales pages list discounts up to 70% off on hundreds of new items every week, plus it’s not uncommon to find free shipping promo codes when you spend over a certain amount. They also have lots of rebates available, typically for gift cards or even just plain old cash. We’ll keep you posted on the best deals and promo codes, so check back here often. You can also sign up for Cabela’s emails or texts to receive exclusive offers and coupons. Just know that you can typically only use one promo code at a time.

If you’re truly serious about your outdoor pursuits, you might consider a Cabela’s CLUB Visa card. You’ll earn 2% on all Cabela’s purchases, which can be redeemed for use on future purchases. Plus, CLUB members get access to special offers and rewards.
