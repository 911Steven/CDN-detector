        • Michelle C. wrote a review for Izakaya Sozai
          One minute ago
          11/6/2015

          Favorite Izakaya spot in the Bay!  I'm not a huge ramen person, but the Ritsu Ramen is seriously bomb. Very flavorful with the braised pork perfectly cooked to tenderness, and piping hot when it arrives (I hate it when my soup is lukewarm). The tuna tartare also has a unique twist with lotus root chips and wasabi.  If you're a fan of foie, you'll have to order the monkfish liver with strawberries.  Most of the dishes we ordered were plated beautifully too. Only set back is this place gets pretty packed, so arrive early to avoid the rush!

        • Ryan B. wrote a review for Sophie’s Cuppa Tea
          One minute ago
          11/6/2015

          This is my favorite tea shop in the bay. I'll keep it simple, there are two things I love here. The first is the tea itself. The quality is fantastic - I tried the dragonwell here and it was far better than what I have had at other shops. Second, I love that the owners are so friendly and quick with a recommendation. They have a huge selection which can be overwhelming, but with a question or two about what you like they'll happily recommend something excellent. If you're looking for good tea leaves or to get to know somebody over a cup, look no further!
