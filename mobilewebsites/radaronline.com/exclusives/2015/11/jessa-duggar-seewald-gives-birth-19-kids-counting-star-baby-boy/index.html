It’s A Boy! Jessa Duggar Gives Birth

Is it a boy or a girl? Click to find out!

Posted on Nov 5, 2015 @ 18:59PM

Jessa Duggar Seewald has given birth to her first child, RadarOnline.com has learned.

Mrs. Ben Seewald and her husband welcomed a bouncy baby boy. No other delivery details were immediately available.

The 22-year-old reality star and her husband are going to be the focus, along with her sister Jill Duggar and her husband, Derick Dillard, of a three-part special airing on TLC, the network that canceled their show after their brother Josh Duggar’s scandals  began in the summer of 2015.

Jessa did not reveal the gender of her baby during her pregnancy but dutifully documented her growing baby bump, although to much less fanfare than her sister Jill’s.

Baby Seewald is the sixth grandchild for Michelle and Jim Bob Duggar. The matriarch of the family posted a blog recently reminding women that they must submit to sex with their husbands whenever they’re asked, even if they’re “big pregnant.”

Congratulations to Jessa and Ben on their new bundle of joy.
