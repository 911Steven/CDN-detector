On Wednesday night, Gabrielle Union was seen kicking off her birthday festivities and hubby Dwyane Wade’s season opening win with friends at Hyde AmericanAirlines Arena. Union, whose birthday would officially begin at midnight, made the courtside lounge her stomping grounds, enjoying a pre-game dinner and returning again to HYDE to celebrate during halftime. During the game Union enthusiastically cheered on her husband (yes, she really is his biggest fan), and as soon as the game concluded, they retreated back to the in-arena hotspot for a little post-game party while she waited for her man to finish interviews and his post-game routine. Union’s friends surprised her with an oversized cake donning an elementary school photo of the actress and multiple bottles of Moet. Just when she thought her surprises were over, Mario, who sang the national anthem earlier that evening, jumped into the DJ booth and led the venue in singing Happy Birthday to Union who turned 43 at midnight!
