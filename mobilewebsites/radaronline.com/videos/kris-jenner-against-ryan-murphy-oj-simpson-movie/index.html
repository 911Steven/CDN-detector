Kris Jenner is freaking out over the upcoming Ryan Murphy miniseries about O.J. Simpson after learning that the uber-producer has decided to turn her character into a major role in the series.

“Kris is panicking over this and is desperate for intel on the plot,” an insider tells RadarOnline.com.
