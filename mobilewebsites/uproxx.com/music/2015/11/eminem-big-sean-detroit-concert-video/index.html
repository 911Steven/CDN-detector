Watch Detroit Go Wild When Big Sean Brought Eminem Out On Stage Friday Night

By: Dariel Figueroa 11.08.15

Subscribe to UPROXX

Share This Video

Eminem is not one to make regular appearances, both at other artists’ concerts or in the media. That’s why it was a special occasion Friday night, when Big Sean held an encore performance of “Detroit vs. Everybody” featuring Dej Loaf, Danny Brown, Royce da 5’9,” Trick-Trick, and, of course, Eminem.

During the “Finding Paradise” concert performance, each contributor of the track strolled out on stage for their part, but when Eminem’s verse arrived, the Detroit icon rose from the stage and blasted the audience with his trademark, rapid-fire cadence; the crowd exploded in response. Dressed in his usual garb — a hoodie and sweatpants — Em proceeded to rip the mic as the other Detroit artists stood back, in awe of the Motor City madman.

This was Big Sean’s first concert in his hometown of Detroit in two years. Check out the video above and try not to get goosebumps when Marshall Mathers sets the stage on fire.
