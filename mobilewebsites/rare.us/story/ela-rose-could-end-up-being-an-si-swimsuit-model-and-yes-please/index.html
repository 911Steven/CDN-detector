Why yellow? Rare.us is "going yellow" Nov. 5-11 in support of veterans causes. You may be familiar with the yellow "Support our Troops" ribbon. To Rare, yellow reminds us of the courage and valor of our veterans, and by transforming our look this week from red to yellow, we hope to draw more attention to the causes close to veterans.

We are partnering with Team Red, White and Blue to tell the amazing stories of these men and women who are thriving and making a difference in their communities every day, and to remind America that veterans are not victims. Team Red, White and Blue's mission is to enrich the lives of America's veterans by connecting them to their communities through physical and social activity.

Learn more about how you can support our veterans, donate to the cause (Rare will match up to $10,000 in donations), and follow the conversation this week at #rarecares
